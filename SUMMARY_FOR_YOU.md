## Is It the "World's Best"?

**NO** - Because:

1. **No universal "best"** exists in software
   - Different tools for different jobs
   - Horses vs. cars vs. planes

2. **It's a framework, not a language**
   - Language: JavaScript
   - Framework: Express
   - DSL: easy.js
   - Can't compare differently-scoped things

3. **Other tools win at their specialties**
   - Java: Enterprise reliability
   - Rust: Performance & safety
   - Python: Data science
   - Go: Cloud tooling

**BUT**: easy.js IS the best at what it does (API development)
